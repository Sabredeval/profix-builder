About Profix Builders

Learn about our journey, our team, and our commitment to excellence in construction

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Profix Builders began as a small team of dedicated craftsmen with a passion for quality construction. What started as a specialized loft conversion company quickly evolved into a comprehensive construction service as our reputation for excellence spread throughout London.

Our founder, Bohdan Makalus, built the company on core principles of transparency, quality craftsmanship, and client satisfaction. Today, these values remain at the heart of everything we do, from initial consultation to project completion.

We take pride in transforming properties across London, creating spaces that enhance both aesthetics and functionality while adding significant value to our clients' investments.
